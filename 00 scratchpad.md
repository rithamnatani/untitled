backstory:

A man who spent nearly eighty years as a slave dies in betrayal.

Born in chains, he lived his entire life in fear. He obeyed every order, worked until his body broke, and endured daily beatings without resistance. Though hatred burned in his heart, he was too weak and afraid to act. The one woman he cared for was taken by his master, and he could do nothing but watch.

When rumors spread of a hidden artifact in the mines, the slave master promised freedom to whichever slave found it first. By cruel fate, the old slave was the one who discovered it. Foolishly clinging to hope, he handed it over, believing he had finally earned his freedom.

Instead, the master activated the artifact, seized its power, and murdered him to keep the secret for himself.

As he lay dying in the dark, the old slave made one final wish: that he might return and take revenge on everyone who had crushed his life.

He awakens in the past, reborn as his own five-year-old self—still a slave, still in chains, but now with the memories of eighty years of suffering and the knowledge of where the artifact lies.

This time, he takes it for himself.

Yet the artifact does not immediately grant him power. Its spirit deems his young body too weak to bear its inheritance, forcing him to train in secret for ten long years while hiding his true intentions from his masters.

At fifteen, he finally meets its requirements and inherits a legendary body-refining art, becoming a terrifying physical cultivator whose strength eclipses the local slave-owning clan.

Then, after a lifetime in chains and a death born of betrayal, he begins his revenge.

He slaughters the family that enslaved him, tears down their rule, and from the ruins founds a new sect for the broken, the abandoned, and the enslaved—a sect built not on bloodline or privilege, but on rebirth through suffering.
