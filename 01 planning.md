# Planning

## Current Direction

- medium: light novel first, manhua later
- genre lane: progression fantasy / xianxia / reincarnator setup
- keep things flexible for now
- avoid locking lore too early

## What Seems Strong So Far

- male protagonist
- reincarnator angle
- zero to hero progression
  - good slave at first
- artifact as an early turning point
  - artifact (angels tear or something), can only be broken with over 1000 jin strength, takes from slave to heavenly rank.
- classical Chinese inspired setting
- cultivation with qi
- body is trained traditionally

## What Is Still Open

- why the protagonist reincarnated
- what the artifact actually is
- whether the story starts in a clan, sect, city, battlefield, or village
- whether the tone is more ruthless, adventurous, political, or mythic
- how far the body-cultivation side matters compared to qi

## Good Early Priorities

1. decide the protagonist's starting social position
2. decide what the artifact does better than normal cultivation
3. decide the first meaningful conflict
4. decide what makes this setting distinct from generic xianxia
