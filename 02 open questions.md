# Open Questions

## Protagonist

- full reincarnation or partial memory awakening?
- talented but suppressed, or genuinely weak at the start?
- calm strategist, ruthless climber, or stubborn survivor?

## Artifact

- inheritance item, broken relic, sealed spirit, or inner world treasure?
- does it accelerate cultivation, improve comprehension, repair talent, or hide the user?
- what cost or danger keeps it from solving everything?

## Setting

- more historical-feeling imperial world or more mythic cultivation world?
- strong state power, strong sect power, or a balance of both?
- grounded regional story or very large multi-realm story?

## Cultivation

- should qi / soul / body be parallel systems or one main system with side branches?
- should every realm have a clear visual identity for later manhua adaptation?
- should talent grade be fixed at birth or alterable through opportunity and risk?

## First Arc

- humiliation and comeback?
- survival and escape?
- academy or sect entry?
- revenge setup?
- inheritance trial?

## Things To Avoid For Now

- locking the final power ceiling too early
- making the realm ladder too bloated before the first arc exists
- deciding all factions before the protagonist has a clear starting point
